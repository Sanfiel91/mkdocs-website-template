# Selected case studies

These projects show how I approach business problems that require more than a prototype. The focus is on measurable outcomes, maintainable architecture, and production-minded delivery across automation, retrieval, and AI platform work.

Each case study includes the business challenge, the system shape, key design decisions, and the production results that mattered.

<div class="grid cards featured-card" markdown>

-   [AI-Powered Email Automation System](projects/agentic-email-automation.md)

    ---

    ---
